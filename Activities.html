Meet the Pandas

One of our main and biggest attractions is Meet the Pandas. This event gives you and your loved ones a chance to actually come close to a panda and take pictures with it.

The safety of our customers is very important thus we will ensure that we will do everything in our power to make this attraction as safe as possible.

This attraction is only available on weekends during certain times given below:

Ticket Prices - Rs.1000

Times Available - 10am - 3pm

Days Available - Saturday/Sunday

Maximum People Allowed Per Hour - 8

Furthermore, Dehiwala Zoolgical Gardens are working with Zoo's in China to give our visitors chances to go to China and volunteer with Pandas.

For more information on this refer THIS SITE and contact us through our contact us page.

Aquarium

One of the biggest accomplishments of the Dehiwala Zoo is its world class Aquarium. With its unique selection of marine plants and animals it was one of the most expensive projects of the Zoo.

The Aquarium itself is so large that it will take a family at least 5 hours to completely explore it. The Aquarium is definitely one of the most recommended attractions in the Zoo.


Details:

Ticket Prices - Free

Times Available - 10am - 5pm

Days Available - Everyday

For more information on the Dehiwala Zoological Gardens Aquarium refer THIS SITE.

Reptile House

Our reptile house holds all kind of reptiles from tiny house reptiles to crocodiles. The reptile house was a very early project started by the Zoo. Initially it was meant to hold wild crocodiles captured around the country which are believed to be a threat to humans.

Afterwards, due to the increase in demand for the crocodiles the Zoo decided on building its reptile house. Whether you are an enthusiast for reptiles or if you just want to get an upclose picture with a crocodile the reptile house is definitely worth visiting. There will be active photographers in the reptile house to take pictures and capture the memories you make.


Details:

Ticket Prices - Free

Times Available - 10am - 5pm

Days Available - Everyday

Dehiwala Zoo is always looking to find reptiles to its reptile house. Therefore, if you encounter a reptile which you believe is a threat to you and your loved ones please contact us. Furthermore, refer to THIS SITE for more information for your safety.
