ABOUT US

The Dehiwala National Zoo is one of the most well recognized zoos. Often called Colombo Zoo, the Dehiwala Zoo is one of the oldest zoos in Asia.

The Zoo has become famous not only because of all the exotic species of plants and animals it holds but also because of its lavished greenery. The Dehiwala Zoo has always promoted conservation of endangered species while maintaining cruel free conditions.

Annually, over one and a half million people visit the Dehiwala Zoo. The Zoo has also been visited by over 2000 students from universities and schools for educational purposes in the last year.

Currently the zoo is going through major redevelopment processes to create a better image in the eyes of the public and to create better living conditions for the animals and plants held in the zoo.



The Zoo currently houses over:


  • 72 Species of Mammals
  • 65 Species of Birds
  • 31 Species of Reptiles
  • 89 Species of Fish
  • 3 Families of Amphibians
  • 30 Species of Butterflies






Vision:

To be one of the best zoological gardens in the world

Mission:

House and display a diversified healthy collection of wild animal populations, under utmost care, through a code of best practices, state of the art technology and expertise in par with modern zoo concepts.

Upcoming...

With the pace at which the world is changing it is difficult to keep up. The Zoo is currently world class. However, there is always room for more improvement which is why the Zoo has made a decision to go through a redevelopment.

The Dehiwala Zoo is currently going through some major revelopment programs to ensure that you and your loved ones get the best experience possible after the pandemic. The Zoo may be closed for a short period of time while the redevelopment is underway. However, it is important to remember that the redevelopment is done to help ensure that both you and the animals at the zoo have the most comfortable environment possible.
